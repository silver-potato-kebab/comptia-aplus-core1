Exam Strategy

Domain 5 (Hardware and Network Troubleshooting, 28%) and Domain 3 (Hardware, 25%) together account for over half the exam. Prioritize scenario-based objectives — the majority of questions are performance-based and require applying knowledge, not just recalling it.

About This Guide

Each objective page provides in-depth coverage aligned to the 220-1201 V15 exam objectives document (Version 4.0). Objectives are tagged by question stem verb — Scenario, Compare, Explain, or Summarize — which mirrors the wording used on the actual exam. Your progress is saved in this browser automatically.
